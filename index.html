Questions

Good questions.

Is this another pet translator gimmick?
No. We do not translate barks into words. 4Feet analyzes behavioral patterns across multiple modalities — posture, vocalizations, movement — grounded in peer-reviewed ethological research. This is behavioral science, not a party trick.
How is this different from MeowTalk?
MeowTalk focuses on cat vocalizations only. 4Feet covers dogs and cats, analyzes the full spectrum of behavior (not just sounds), and includes health monitoring, vet reports, and personalized pet profiles.
Do I need special hardware?
No. 4Feet works with just your phone camera. The optional smart collar adds continuous monitoring and health precision, but every core feature works without it.
Is the data private?
Live scans run entirely on your device. No raw video is stored. Cloud storage is opt-in only, encrypted, and you control deletion.
