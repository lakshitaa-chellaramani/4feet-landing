You're probably wondering...

Is this another gimmick pet translator?

Nope. We don't turn barks into English sentences. 4Feet analyzes real behavioral patterns. Body language, vocalizations, movement. All grounded in peer-reviewed research. Think of it as behavioral science in your pocket, not a party trick.

How is this different from MeowTalk?

MeowTalk only does cat sounds. 4Feet covers both dogs and cats, reads the full spectrum of behavior (not just audio), and includes health monitoring, vet reports, and profiles that learn your specific pet over time.

Do I need to buy a collar?

Nope! 4Feet works with just your phone camera. The optional smart collar adds continuous health monitoring, but every core feature works without it.

Is my pet's data safe?

Scans run on your device. No video is stored unless you choose to save it. Everything is encrypted. You control what gets kept and what gets deleted.
